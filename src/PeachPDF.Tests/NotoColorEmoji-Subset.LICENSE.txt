NotoColorEmoji-Subset.ttf is a subset (a handful of common emoji) of the COLRv1
build of "Noto Color Emoji", Copyright 2013 Google LLC, licensed under the SIL
Open Font License, Version 1.1 (OFL-1.1). It uses COLR (version 1) + CPAL color
glyphs over `glyf` outlines (no bitmap tables).

The font is bundled solely as a test/showcase asset to demonstrate and verify
COLR/CPAL color-glyph rendering. Only a small range of glyphs is retained via
fonttools subset; the color glyphs are otherwise unmodified. The full OFL text
follows.
