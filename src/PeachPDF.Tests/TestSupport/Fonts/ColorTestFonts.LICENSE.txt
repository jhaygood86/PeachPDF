ColorTestV0.ttf and ColorTestV1.ttf are original, hand-authored test fixtures
generated by generate_color_fonts.py (in this directory). They contain no
third-party font data and are released into the public domain (CC0). They exist
solely to exercise PeachPDF's COLR/CPAL color-glyph rendering in the test suite
and showcase; they are not intended for text rendering.
